About Us

Who We Are

Noor Clinic Laboratory is a state-of-the-art medical facility located in Sidi Bel Abbès, Algeria. We specialize in providing accurate and timely diagnostic services to help healthcare providers deliver the best possible care to their patients.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality and precision in all our tests and analyses.

Our Mission

We utilize cutting-edge technology to deliver comprehensive laboratory testing, including hematology, biochemistry, microbiology, and molecular diagnostics.
